# Frequently Asked Questions

## What is Flarial?
Flarial is a custom client for Minecraft Bedrock Edition that enhances your gameplay experience.

## Is Flarial free?
Yes, Flarial is completely free to use.

## Does it work with the latest Minecraft version?
We constantly update Flarial to maintain compatibility with the latest Minecraft versions.

## Is it safe to use?
Flarial is developed with security in mind and doesn't modify any game files.
